About LCC Learning Communities


The concept of "learning communities" at Lower Colmbia College began in the late 1980's under the direction of retired LCC philosophy instructor Jerry Zimmerman. Since then, the program has grown to include Integrated Studies and I-Trans courses taught by instructors representing many of LCC's academic programs. For more, see this 2012 story from The Daily News on how and why Zimmerman and his colleagues introduced this effective method of teaching at LCC.
